A story the brain tells itself

To me the most likely explanation of the *soft* problem of why we have a consciousness is that the brain needs to be "used by itself", so it tells itself a story about the world that surrounds it. The story is not told in English, but in the language of qualia.

There are several reasons why the brain would like to use itself and not merely 'function' directly. One being that reality is full of strongly emergent phenomena (we will talk about this in a future post), and there is no prior learned experience that can fully prepare us to navigate a new strongly emergent phenomenon.
