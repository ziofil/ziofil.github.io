What is consciousness without free will?

It seems to me that consciousness has no meaning without free will. I have no idea how to instantiate free will just like I have no idea how to instantiate consciousness, but it seems illogical to waste resources on running the mind's eye only to have it passively spectate an everchanging present.

I understand why it is so difficult to make space for free will in our current understanding of the physical world, but I also cannot sweep under the rug that consciousness would need to find a new meaning if it cannot be to guide the emergence of actions.
